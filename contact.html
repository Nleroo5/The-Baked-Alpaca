Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I order?

For custom cakes and wedding orders, we recommend booking 3-6 months in advance. For smaller orders and pastries, 2-3 weeks notice is preferred, though we can sometimes accommodate shorter timelines depending on availability.

Do you offer tastings?

Yes! We offer complimentary tasting sessions for wedding cake orders. For other custom cakes, tastings can be arranged for a small fee that is credited toward your final order.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We offer gluten-free, dairy-free, and vegan options. Please let us know about any dietary requirements when placing your order so we can ensure your cake is both delicious and safe for all your guests.

Do you deliver?

Yes, we offer delivery and setup services for wedding cakes and large orders throughout Peachtree City, Fayette County, and surrounding areas. Delivery fees vary based on location and order size.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require a 50% deposit to secure your order date. Deposits are non-refundable but can be transferred to a different date with at least 2 weeks notice. Full cancellations within 2 weeks of the event date forfeit the deposit.
