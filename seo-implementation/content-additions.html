Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I order a custom cake?

For custom celebration cakes, we recommend ordering at least 2-3 weeks in advance. For wedding cakes, we recommend booking 3-6 months ahead to ensure availability for your date and allow time for consultations and tastings.

Do you offer wedding cake tastings?

Yes! We offer private tasting consultations for wedding cake clients. During your tasting, you'll sample various cake flavors, fillings, and frostings while discussing your design vision. Contact us at (470) 800-3825 to schedule your consultation.

What areas do you serve?

We are based in Peachtree City, GA and serve the greater Atlanta metropolitan area including Fayetteville, Newnan, Tyrone, and surrounding communities. For events outside this area, please contact us to discuss delivery options.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we can accommodate various dietary needs including gluten-free and vegan options. Please discuss your specific requirements during your consultation so we can create the perfect custom solution for your event.

Where can I find your mobile bakery?

During market season, our vintage horse trailer bakery appears at local farmers markets and events throughout Georgia. Follow us on Instagram @thebakedalpaca or Facebook for our current schedule and locations.
