Our Story

Where European tradition meets Southern hospitality

From Passion to Perfection

The Baked Alpaca was born from a simple belief: that every celebration deserves something extraordinary. We combine traditional European baking techniques with artistic innovation to create luxury baked goods that are as beautiful as they are delicious.

What started as a passion for pastry has grown into a beloved bakery serving Peachtree City and beyond. Our signature vintage horse trailer brings European-inspired treats to local markets, while our custom cake design services help make weddings and special events truly unforgettable.

Every creation that leaves our bakery is made with premium ingredients, meticulous attention to detail, and a commitment to excellence that our clients have come to trust.

Our Vintage Horse Trailer

During market season, you'll find us operating out of our beautifully renovated vintage horse trailer. This mobile bakery brings the charm of European patisseries to farmers markets and events throughout Georgia.

Stop by to discover fresh pastries, seasonal treats, and our signature creations. It's the perfect way to experience The Baked Alpaca magic in your local community.
